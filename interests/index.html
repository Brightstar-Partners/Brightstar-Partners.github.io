Areas of Interest

The following headings give a flavour of our focus. But if you have exciting IP based technologies, contact us we are always interested to see how they can fit with our portfolio, approach and investment strategies.

Technology

Medical devices, diagnostics and digital services. Preferably with a strong IP base.

Both consumer and professional care markets.

We are looking for disruptive technologies that change both dramatically change the price point and improve patient outcomes.

Geography

Brightstar mainly invests in companies in South Korea or the UK with the aim of championning them into the European and Asian markets.

We are also interested in working with in UK based companies with a strong Asian market perspective.

Stage

We are more concerned about the potential to disrupt markets than the stage of the company and are happy to look at opportunities from early stage IP to late stage product companies if we feel we can add value to the company.
