KUKI-I Fund

The KUKI-I fund was launched in October 2015, closing with $41m of financing.

Jointly managed by Brightstar Partners and Kiwoom Investments its investment strategy is split equally between:

  • IP spin-outs
  • Round A investments
  • Near-IPO investments

Investments must have a strong Korean angle, preferably a Korean company. There needs to be a strong link to the UK, preferably UK sourced licence or technology.

The focus area is medical devices, diagnostics and digital systems.

This fund is now fully invested.
