About Us

Brightstar Partners utilises a business model that it developed for creating internationally trading businesses built by spinning technology out from corporate and institutional laboratories.

The Brightstar Partners team combines individuals with a long track record in technology investments with entrepreneurs who have successfully built multiple companies. It works closely with health institutions such as Teaching and Research Hospitals and their associated Universities in Korea and the UK to source technologies developed by those at the cutting edge of modern medicine.

It combines these technologies with a well developed incubation process for building companies, and uses its network of Hospitals to find early customers and participants in clinical trials for the resulting products.
