Brightstar Partners

Brightstar Partners are an international management consultancy and venture capital firm with offices in the UK, Australia and Korea.

The Brightstar team has a long history in early stage technology spin outs from corporate labs and institutions. The team works in a hands-on operational mode to help the entrepreneurs internationalise and build their companies rapidly.

Brightstar Strategy

Brightstar works with companies and funds that would benefit from the international links it has pioneered in the healthcare industry.

Brightstar specialises in early stage, intellectual property rich start-ups; especially in medical devices, diagnostics and digital systems. These are typically institutional spin outs or have strong connections with a corporate or instituitional R&D facility. Much of Brightstar's work is with government bodies or related instiutions.
